Privacy Policy • Last Updated: June 16, 2026

🛡️ KidzLearny is designed with utmost care for child safety. The app operates 100% offline, contains absolutely NO ads, and does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

1. Introduction

KidzLearny ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our young users and providing a safe, educational, and fun environment. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in the KidzLearny mobile application.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance)

KidzLearny is specifically built for children. We strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR-K) rules:

  • We do NOT collect any personal identifiers (like names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, or locations) from children.
  • No sign-up or registration is required to use the app.

3. Offline Operations & Local Storage

All app activities and progress are processed directly on your device. The only data saved is stored locally using the device's secure storage (SharedPreferences):

  • Reward Progress: The number of stars and badges earned by the child to track progress locally.
  • App Settings: Sound and music toggle states.

This data stays on the device and is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

4. App Permissions & Services

The app only requests standard features required for core operations:

  • Text-to-Speech (TTS): Utilized locally to read out alphabets, numbers, fruits, animals, shapes, and colors for auditory learning. This does not record or capture any voice data.
  • Internet Access: Only used if an adult unlocks the Parent Verification section and manually chooses to launch the support email client or open this Privacy Policy page. No internet communication happens in the background.

5. Third-Party Advertisements & Analytics

To ensure a completely safe and uninterrupted learning experience for kids:

  • We have NO advertisements inside the app.
  • We do NOT use third-party tracking or behavioral analytics tools.
